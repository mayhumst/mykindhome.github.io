Welcome :)

Here at My Kind Home, we are dedicated to teaching kids about the importance of environmental conservation and the simple steps they can take to make a positive impact on the planet.

Our mission is to educate and empower young minds to become stewards of the environment and to protect our planet for generations to come.

Through engaging and interactive resources, we aim to introduce young people to the interconnectedness of all living things and the ways in which our actions can have significant impacts on the environment.

About Our Team

We are a group of undergraduate students at NYU. We are dedicated to contributing our technical skills towards innovative solutions to one of humanity's greatest threats: the climate crisis.
